Wedding Photography For Modern Couples

Many weddings ago, my wife Christine encouraged me to take my passion for photography and follow my dreams of pursuing wedding photography.  She and I have both always loved weddings and she encouraged me to take the step into really turning my obsession with photography technique and our mutual love of hospitality and all things love related into a full time job.  Since then, we've worked on turning our candid style of documentary photography into beautiful art photography and wedding day portraits. 

We love love, and celebrate love in all it's forms, regardless of race, religion, or orientation. 

Families and Babies

Our fun loving demeanor and problem solving nature serves us well in documenting any kind of family gathering, to showcase the special togetherness life's special moments, such as birthdays, bar mitzvahs, or hospital birth portraits.  We feel so fortunate that so many people have let us share and record their family celebrations, and are blessed each time one of our images becomes a cherished family memory. 

I've been a baby guy since I was volunteering with my mother in the church nursery, and I've been blessed with three adorable little nephews.  Christine and I only have four legged babies right now, so we make up for it by getting to play with everyone else's newborns!  We're  excellent with kids of all ages, and can capture your little devils looking their most angelic.
